JJ Kaufman was born and raised on Manhattan's Upper West Side in New York City.
He attended public school throughout his childhood and teenage years attending PS 75, Booker T. Washington Middle School and The Bronx High School of Science.

During this time he participated in musical activities such as Chorus, Piano, Cello and performed in two rock bands.

Upon turning 18 he moved to Montreal to study Mathematics at McGill University. He joined an a ccapella group on campus named Tonal Ecstacy and would make music at home as a hobby.

After graduating from McGill in 2012, he taught himself how to produce music using Ableton live. In addition to this he began learning French.

In 2016, Four years after beginning to learn French, he began studying Musique Numeriques (Digital Music) at the Faculty of Music at the University of Montreal entirely in French. During this time he would become bilingual and gain experience in sound design, music composition, music programming and sound recording and engineering.

Having Recently Completed the program in the spring of 2019, he is now ready to start a career utilizing his passion for Sound and Music.
